Cashglade

Cashglade is a practice ground for money skills — save, invest, and earn without any real money on the line.

🔗 Live app: https://samiderincg.github.io/CashGlade/

What it does

Cashglade is a single-page web app built around four ideas:


📈 Simulate — Trade a simulated market across stocks, crypto, commodities, forex, and futures with $10,000 in practice cash. Real charts are pulled live from TradingView so you can study actual price action while trading your simulated portfolio.
📚 Learn — Short, plain-language lessons on saving, compound interest, investing basics, risk across asset classes, growing your income, and budgeting.
🎮 Games — Three quick games that teach money skills by feel, not lecture:

Budget Survivor — allocate a monthly income across 12 months and survive random life events
Lemonade Stand — price and staff a tiny business day by day
Compound Machine — sliders that show how contributions, rate, and time compound over the years



🤖 AI Coach — Ask an AI anything about saving, investing, or how the markets in the simulator actually work. Educational only, not personalized financial advice. (Currently being wired up — coming soon.)


No real money is ever used or at risk anywhere in the app.

Tech stack


Single-file HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no build step, no framework
TradingView Widget for live chart data
Anthropic's Claude API for the AI Coach (in progress)
Hosted for free on GitHub Pages


Running it locally

Just open index.html in a browser — no install or build step needed. Everything works except the AI Coach, which is still being set up.

Status

Actively in progress. The Simulate, Learn, and Games sections are fully working. The AI Coach is being wired up next.
